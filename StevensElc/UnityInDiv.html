Footstompers International Dance Organization.
Footstompers International Dance Organization also known as FIDO is an on campus club where culture meets dance lovers. The idea behind the group is bring together likeminded people from various cultures and provide them a platform to think out of the box, and innovate a dance form that has never been heard or seen before and that is educates viewers about cultural practices, ideologies and interests followed in a culture and community and also help them realize value of teamspirit, and unity in real world by working as team to innovate a form, tackle the issues and reap the pleasure

The group was introduced in Fall'15 and is now 16 months old, and as we are young there is plenty of room for creativity, and innovation and we highly encourage students to join the group and pursue their passion for dance once in a while to take a break, pursue their interest and let the world realize that it's the creativity with academic excellence what's makes ducks so special.

Thanks !
- Team FIDO Fall'16

Student Language Exchange Club
Student Language Exchange Club also known as SLEC is an on campus club where we organize events to accumulate grad students from totally different countries and cultures to collaborate with each other, kinda like open discussions on almost any topic.

Additionally, we share our knowledge on different cultures, some standard etiquettes in the States and ask to put their input into it as well to spread a word of knowledge, we (officials) also make sure to engage them with extra activities like games, music and movie to entertain them whilst having fun of meeting new people, and of course free food.

Furthermore, in Fall '16, we organized an event of food festival event under International Education month for entire Stevens Community where we managed to bring food from 8 different countries and to our surprise, we had a head count of 250+ individuals. We couldn't thank OGSA and Stevens community with volunteers enough for their constant support and participation without them it won't be possible. We were overwhelmed to be a part of it.

Thanks !
- Team SLEC Fall '16
